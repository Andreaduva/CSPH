ACTUAL FORCE/DENSITY COMPUTATIONS: 
  - Density sum performed on all marker types EXCEPT for WALL bce. Contributions to density summation ONLY from PURE FLUID markers.
  - RHS of EOM computed for ALL markers types EXCEPT for WALL bce (all derivatives set to zero by default). Avoided individual SOLID-SOLID contributions.
    So a RIGID bce will see its properties evolved in time, due only due to neighboring fluid markers. 
    a Fluid marker will see its properties evolved in time also with RIGID/WALL contributions.


- PERMEABLE BOUNDARY CONDITIONS STILL A ISSUE, CHRONO AFAIK NOT READY TO DEAL WITH GHOST BUFFER DOMAIN.
- SOLID BOUNDARY CONDITIONS FOR NOW IMPLEMENTED CLASSIC ADAMI BCS WITH HIS SUGGESTIONS FOR FREE SLIP CONDITION.
  MEANS EXTRAPOLATE P, U, H AND RHO FROM EOS. FOR VELOCITY USE PRESCRIBED ONE ONLY. THEN AVOID VISCOUS INTERACTION 
  BETWEEN FLUID PARTICLES AND SURROINDING DUMMY ONES. THIS IMPLICITELY ENFORCE NOPENETRATION.
  IMPLEMENTED WITH AN IF STATEMENT IN COMPUTATIONS OF RHS OF DERIVATIVES.

IN THE DENSITY EULER STEP PRESSURE IS IMMEDIATELY UPDATED AFTER DENSITY INTEGRATION. MAKES SENSE SINCE OTHER RHS OF DERIVATIVES ARE ALREADY COMPUTED.
